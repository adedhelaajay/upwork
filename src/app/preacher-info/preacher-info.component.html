Bro. Krupadanam Adedhela

Bro. Krupadanam was born in Tandoor and brought up in Hyderabad. He worked in the government and retired as Deputy Director of Agriculture. His parents, the late Mr. A.P. Israel and Mrs. Rathnamma, raised him in a Methodist denominational background. Bro. Krupadanam is married to Anna Christina and blessed with four children.
In 1978, he was introduced to the true gospel of Jesus Christ by the late Bro. Prakasam Pulaga. Since then, he left denominationalism, obeyed the gospel, and was added to the Lord's Church.
